Protein Baked Rice

Perfect when you have rice leftover! Serve as side dish or main for lunch or dinner.

The recipe is very simple and quick to prepare. Packed with protein and health carbs. You can use just plain white rice or you can mix in some other ingredients like sweet peas, sausage, broccoli, etc.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice to fill a baking dish
  • Filling to mix in of your preference. I'll be using sweet peas (cooked).
  • Cottage cheese. Enough to cover the rice.
  • Grated parmesan cheese
  • Chives

Steps

  1. Heat up your airfryer or oven at 350F.
  2. Mix the cooked rice with the sweet peas (or any other ingredient of your choice).
  3. Put the mix in a baking dish
  4. Cover with cottage cheese of your choice (I'll be using a 2% fat for lower calories).
  5. Top with some parmesan cheese and chives

Bake for 15min or when it's golden. Enjoy!
